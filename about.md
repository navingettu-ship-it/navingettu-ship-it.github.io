# About Daily Health Picks

We started this site because the supplement industry is full of noise — inflated claims, paid reviews, and products that don't deliver.

Our goal is simple: give you honest, research-backed reviews that help you make smarter purchasing decisions.

## How We Review

Every product we cover is evaluated on:

- **Ingredient quality** — are the active compounds dosed at clinically studied amounts?
- **Third-party testing** — is the product verified by USP, NSF, or Informed Sport?
- **Value** — does the price reflect the quality, or are you paying for marketing?
- **Real user feedback** — we analyse patterns across hundreds of customer reviews, not just the highlights

## Affiliate Disclosure

This site participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. When you click a link and make a purchase, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This never influences our reviews — we only recommend products we'd stand behind regardless.
